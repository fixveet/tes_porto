FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Questions from students and grads like you, answered honestly.

Is it really free to start? +
Yes! You can create and publish a full portfolio completely free. Our free plan includes one portfolio, three projects, and a public shareable link. No credit card required to sign up.
I have no work experience. Can I still use this? +
Absolutely — that's exactly who we built this for. You can showcase campus projects, organizational roles, personal projects, coursework, and volunteer experiences. Our guided storytelling framework helps you present any experience in a compelling, professional way.
Can I update my portfolio anytime? +
Yes, you can update your portfolio any time. All changes are auto-saved and published in real-time. Your link never changes, so anyone who has it will always see your latest version.
Is this suitable for non-designers and non-coders? +
100%! Portolio is designed for all majors — from business to communications to STEM. You don't need any design or coding skills. Just answer the guided prompts, pick a template, and your portfolio is ready.
Can I export my portfolio as a PDF? +
Yes! You can export your portfolio as a clean, professional PDF at any time. Great for emailing directly to recruiters or uploading to job portals that require document uploads.
What's the CCI storytelling framework? +
CCI stands for Context → Contribution → Impact. It's a proven storytelling method adapted from how top professionals at consulting firms and tech companies present their work. It helps you write experience descriptions that are clear, specific, and memorable — instead of vague job descriptions.
